### Tu sweep is the definitive test

The 5.5× Tu_inf change span 0.18%-1.0% covers the practically-relevant range for low-turbulence aerodynamics (Ladson LTPT was at the lower bound; Tu>1% would no longer match Ladson conditions). The transition model IS responding to Tu — Cd@α=0° moved 5.4% and the α=0° transient shape was visibly different — but the HEADLINE Cl@α=8° did not move at all. This is because at α=8° the favorable pressure gradient on the suction side drives transition to x/c<0.05 regardless of Tu_inf, leaving 95% of the BL turbulent.

iter3 with Tu=0.5% (between iter1 and iter2) would land between iter1 and iter2 — same null result. iter4 would not move the answer. The 2-iter ceiling judgment is methodologically sound.
